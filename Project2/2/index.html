           only            some            of            us,            the            ones            living            in            tunnels,            eating            crickets            to            survive            when            the            rest            had            died           


from            mass            starvation            after            droughts            lasted            longer            and            seas            rose            faster            and            wars            killed            bigger            because            everyone


wanted            what            little            was           left.            I'd            be            fine            with            being            one            of            the            billions            dead            unless            you            were            still           


alive.            Under            a            down            comforter            or            by            a            trash            fire,            I            want            to            be            where            you            are.            You            know           


how            poorly            I            dig            holes,            how            angry            I            get            when            I'm            cold,            how            twice            I've            accidentally            maced            myself,
