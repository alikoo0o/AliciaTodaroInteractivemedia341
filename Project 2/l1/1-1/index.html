The scientist on the radio said that humans

               will survive, and at first I was buoyed,

                     but she meant only some of us,

                       the ones living in tunnels, eating crickets

                            to survive when the rest had died from

                               mass starvation after droughts lasted longer

                                   and seas rose faster and wars killed bigger

                                       because everyone wanted what little was left.

                                         I'd be fine with being one of the billions

                                                  dead unless you were still alive.
